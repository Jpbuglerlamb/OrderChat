Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how JP AI Platform collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the platform, including chat ordering, restaurant dashboards, QR ordering, menu tools, and AI optimiser features.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information such as:

• account details, including name, email address, phone number, and business information
• restaurant details, including address, opening hours, menus, and uploaded files
• customer order details, including ordered items, timestamps, basket contents, and order summaries
• customer contact details provided during ordering or checkout, such as name, email address, or phone number
• uploaded order history files, menu files, images, PDFs, CSVs, and related business data
• technical and usage data needed to operate, secure, and improve the platform

2. How we use information

We use information to operate and improve the platform, including to:

• power chat ordering and route orders to the relevant restaurant or business
• manage accounts, menus, dashboards, QR tools, and settings
• provide support, monitor performance, diagnose issues, and maintain security
• process uploaded files and generate structured menu or order data
• improve service quality, product features, and platform reliability

3. Order data and business optimisation

We may use menu data, live order data, and uploaded order history to generate analytics, trends, and operational insights for the relevant business using the platform.

This may include best-selling item analysis, pairing suggestions, timing patterns, sales trends, menu performance signals, and other AI optimiser outputs designed to help the restaurant or business understand and improve its operations.

4. Payments

If you purchase a paid plan, payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. We do not store full payment card details directly on our own systems where a third-party processor is used.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only where necessary to operate the platform, including:

• with the restaurant or business receiving a customer order
• with service providers that help us operate the platform, such as hosting, storage, email, analytics, or payment providers
• where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the security or integrity of the platform

6. Data retention

We retain account, menu, order, and uploaded business data for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and improve service performance.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the business relationship with the platform.

7. Data protection

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. You may also request deletion of your account or associated information by contacting us through the feedback page.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of the platform after updates take effect means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have any privacy questions or requests, please contact us through the feedback page on the platform.
